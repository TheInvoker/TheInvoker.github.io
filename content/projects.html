Legal IQ

Brought to you by team Legal Monkeys, we participated in the first Legal Hackathon in Toronto, to create a solution to significantly reduce research time done by lawyers. In an ever changing and evolving world of technology, technical details in legal documents get increasingly sophisticated, longer, and ultimately more complex and risky. Not only do non-technical lawyers struggle to understand the impact of the details specified in legal documents, but even technology based lawyers at times. There is a wealth of under-utilized information sitting in your content management systems, but nothing there to really analyze and deliver the insights that really leverage the numerical aspect of these areas of your documents.

Let me introduce to you, Legal IQ, an end-to-end solution that specializes in figuring out what the technical language in your document means to you, what it says about your opposition, how it compares to the market environment, and what will likely happen in your case. We combine a number of key technologies that are just booming in our tech industry right now to deliver quality insights to the people who need it the most, your clients. We utilize big data, AI (specifically neural networks), cloud infrastructure, and automated email and document annotation/generation technologies to deliver key insights extremely fast that works with how you work, to make sure you have all the tools you need to make those big decisions. You will never need to second guess yourself when we provide you those facts and statistics as tools to use in your presentations and deliver more value to your clients. Our goal is to turn existing legal professionals who do the grunt work (i.e. legal monkeys) into legal professionals who spend less time digging in documents and more time delivering quality insights.

https://www.osler.com/en/about-us/press-room/2017/osler-lawyers-and-software-developer-participate-i
