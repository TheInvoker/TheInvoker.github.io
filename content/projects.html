Matplotlib Feature Extension, April 2014

My team and I are a small team of students from University of Toronto. We call our team "The Expendables" (from the movie). Our instructor Anya Tafliovich asked us to implement a matplotlib feature as one of our projects for CSCD01 in 2014, so we choose the 3D quiver plot (as requested here and here).

We only had a week to work on the implementation and term report, so it's not as full featured as the 2D quiver. But we got the basics working and it should be usable.

Our pull request is here.
